Overview

PAMBCS Region 02 Overview

What PAMBCS Region 02 Is

The Protected Area Management and Biodiversity Conservation Section (PAMBCS) is the DENR Regional Office II unit that leads technical work on protected areas and biodiversity—translating national law and policy into regional action.

Role within Region 02

PAMBCS Region 02 works with field offices, protected area management boards, and partners to align conservation priorities, standards, and reporting across Cagayan Valley.

Responsibility

The section is responsible for strengthening protected area management and documenting biodiversity outcomes so that decisions in Region 02 are evidence-based, coordinated, and accountable.

Mandate

Mandate and Key Responsibilities

Core responsibilities of PAMBCS Region 02 in protected area management, biodiversity conservation, and regional coordination.

Biodiversity Conservation Programs

Implement and monitor conservation initiatives, targets, and technical standards across Region 02.

Protected Area Governance

Support PAMB decisions, management planning, and site-level governance in line with applicable laws.

Environmental Monitoring & Documentation

Collect, validate, and document biodiversity and protected area data for planning and compliance.

Policy & Program Coordination

Align regional activities with DENR issuances and inter-agency priorities affecting PAs and biodiversity.

Data & Systems Support

Maintain information systems, spatial and tabular records, and reporting workflows for PAMBCS operations.

Functions

Core Functions of PAMBCS Region 02

Main functional areas through which the section delivers its mandate in Region 02.

Protected Area Monitoring

Tracks conditions, uses, and management responses in Region 02’s protected areas to guide interventions and reporting.

Biodiversity Conservation

Advances species and ecosystem conservation programs consistent with national goals and regional priorities.

Data and Systems Management

Curates geospatial and administrative data, quality checks, and systems that underpin transparent PAMBCS work.

Policy and Program Support

Provides technical backstopping, planning inputs, and coordination for policies and programs affecting Region 02 PAs.
